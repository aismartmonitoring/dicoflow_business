📄 Proposal Structure

What to Include in Every Proposal

Follow this exact structure. A well-structured proposal converts 3x better than a plain price quote.

1

Cover Page

DicoFlow logo + Customer centre name + Date + "Prepared by [Your Name]" + Confidential label. Keep it clean and professional.

2

Understanding Your Needs (1 page)

Reference the demo conversation. List their specific pain points: film cost, compliance gaps, manual processes. Show you listened. Use THEIR numbers (studies/day, monthly film spend, staff count).

3

Proposed Solution (1-2 pages)

DicoFlow [Tier Name] — list the top 8-10 features most relevant to THEIR centre. Don't list all 50+ features. Focus on what solves their stated problems. Include 2-3 screenshots.

4

ROI Analysis (1 page)

Copy their numbers from the ROI calculator. Film savings, billing recovery, compliance protection. Show payback period prominently. "DicoFlow pays for itself in [X] months."

5

Investment Summary (1 page)

Licence price → Implementation → AMC → Infrastructure (if applicable). Show Early Adopter discount crossed-out vs regular. Include all 3 payment options. Make the total very clear.

6

Implementation Timeline (half page)

Day 1-2: Server/NAS setup → Day 3-5: Software install + DICOM router → Day 6-7: Training → Day 8: Go-Live → Day 9-14: Support & fine-tuning. Total: 7-14 days.

7

Why DicoFlow? (half page)

4 bullet points: On-premise + Indian compliance + 50% cheaper + One platform. Optionally: quick comparison against the competitor they mentioned during the demo.

8

Next Steps + Terms

"To proceed: Sign this proposal + 50% advance → Implementation starts within 48 hours." Validity: 15 days. Payment terms. AMC starts Year 2. Support included Year 1.

💎 Golden Rules of DicoFlow Pricing

Rule 1: Never discount more than 30%. Add value instead (training, priority support, faster go-live)
Rule 2: Always show ROI before price. Savings anchor the perception of value
Rule 3: Price is one-time, not subscription. Emphasise this — it's DicoFlow's #1 advantage vs cloud vendors
Rule 4: Never pay-what-you-want. Fixed tiers protect margin and set expectations
Rule 5: Walk away from bad deals. A customer who paid too little will always demand too much
Rule 6: Volume discount OR Early Adopter — never both. Pick the one that makes them feel special

❓ FAQ by Tier — Customer Questions

What Customers Ask at Every Stage

Is DicoFlow a subscription or one-time purchase?+

One-time perpetual licence. You own it forever. No monthly fees. Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) starts from Year 2 (15-20% of licence) and covers updates, bug fixes, and support. Year 1 support is included free.

Do I need internet to use DicoFlow?+

No. DicoFlow runs entirely on-premise. Zero internet dependency. Your data stays on YOUR server/NAS. Internet is only needed if you want cloud backup (optional) or DicoFlow managed hosting. Most Indian centres have unreliable internet — DicoFlow works perfectly offline.

How long does implementation take?+

7-14 days. Day 1-2: Server/NAS setup. Day 3-5: Software installation + DICOM router configuration. Day 6-7: Staff training. Day 8: Go-live. Day 9-14: Support during stabilisation period.

Can I start with T1 and upgrade later?+

Yes. You pay only the difference. T1 (₹75K) → T2 costs ₹75K more. T2 (₹1.5L) → T3 costs ₹2L more. T3 → T4 costs ₹4L more. No penalty. Your data, configuration, and patient records carry forward seamlessly.

Is my data secure?+

Bank-grade security: AES-256-GCM encryption at rest, Ed25519 JWT authentication, Argon2id password hashing (same used by cryptocurrency), SHA-512 hash chain for tamper-proof audit trail. Data stays on YOUR server — not in any cloud unless you choose managed hosting.

What hardware do I need?+

T1: Any modern desktop PC. T2-T3: PC + NAS (Synology 2-bay, ~₹40K) or existing server. T4: Server + NAS/SAN for multi-branch. We provide sizing recommendations with the infrastructure calculator — exact specs based on your study volume.

Does T1 include DICOM viewing?+

No. T1 is billing-only: patient registration, worklist, GST e-Invoice, PCPNDT Form F, payment tracking, doctor management, MIS reports. For DICOM viewing, you need T2 or higher. You can upgrade anytime for just ₹75K more.

Can T1 handle multiple modalities?+

Yes. T1 billing supports registering patients for any modality — X-ray, ultrasound, pathology, ECG, etc. It manages the workflow from registration to payment. It just doesn't store or display DICOM images.

What if PCPNDT inspection happens?+

DicoFlow T1 auto-generates Form F with all required fields, maintains the digital register, and can print reports for inspectors instantly. Every entry is timestamp-secured. You'll be the most compliant centre the inspector has seen.

How does DicoFlow receive images from my CT/MRI?+

DicoFlow acts as a DICOM SCP (Service Class Provider). We configure your modality to send images to DicoFlow's IP address. This is standard DICOM C-STORE. Works with ANY modality brand: GE, Siemens, Philips, Canon, Shimadzu, Allengers, etc. Setup takes 15-30 minutes per modality.

Can patients still get physical prints?+

Yes! Film Preparation module creates professional print layouts (10+ templates). You can print to regular printers (paper/A3), DICOM printers, or export as PDF. Most centres reduce film by 70-85% — only printing when the patient specifically asks.

How much storage space do I need?+

Depends on modality: CT averages 200MB/study, MRI 500MB, X-ray 10MB, Ultrasound 30MB. For a typical CT centre doing 40 studies/day: ~240GB/month, ~14.4TB for 5 years (PCPNDT retention). A 16TB NAS (₹55-75K) handles this easily with room to spare.

How does the booklet system work?+

4-page branded booklet: Page 1 (centre logo, patient info, QR), Page 2 (DICOM images in 2×6 grid), Page 3 (structured report with digital signature), Page 4 (centre branding, QR for digital access). 101 configurable fields. Generate + print in 60 seconds. No competitor has this.

Does T3 include voice dictation?+

Yes. Whisper-based local voice dictation with 95%+ accuracy. Runs entirely on-premise (no data sent to cloud). Radiologists can dictate reports directly into structured templates. Supports dictation in English with medical terminology awareness.

How does multi-branch management work?+

Central server manages all branches. Each branch has local DICOM reception + caching. Central dashboard shows real-time status of all branches. Role-based access: HQ admin sees everything, branch admin sees only their branch. Patient records sync across branches automatically.

Does T4 integrate with our existing HIS?+

Yes. T4 includes HL7 v2.x (MLLP) and FHIR R4 integration. Supports ADT (patient registration), ORM (orders), ORU (results), and DICOMweb (WADO-RS, STOW-RS). We'll work with your HIS vendor during implementation to configure the interface.

What is the self-healing architecture?+

7 independent subsystems that auto-detect and recover from failures: Database (auto-repair, WAL recovery), Storage (integrity checks, redundancy), DICOM engine (auto-restart on crash), Queue system (job recovery), Encryption (key rotation, auto-rekey), Audit system (hash chain repair), WebSocket (auto-reconnect). Zero manual intervention for 95% of issues.
