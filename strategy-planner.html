Highest Value

₹6.45L

Target Market: Hospital chains (5+ branches), corporate hospitals, government hospital groups, large diagnostic chains with 200+ studies/day.
  • Month 1-3: Long sales cycle. 10 hospital chain targets identified. On-site demos, CIO meetings. Procurement discussions. Expect 0-1 close in first 3 months. Revenue: ₹0-8.5L
  • Month 4-6: First enterprise deals close. Multi-branch implementation adds complexity. 1 close/month. Revenue: ₹8.5L/mo
  • Month 7-9: Referral from first enterprise. Volume discount negotiation for chains. 1-1.5 closes/month. Revenue: ₹8.5-12.75L/mo
  • Month 10-12: Pipeline acceleration. 1-2 closes/month. Revenue: ₹8.5-17L/mo

⚠️ Warning: T4-only is HIGH RISK. Long sales cycle (3-6 months), procurement delays, cash flow gaps. Never go T4-only unless you have 6-month runway.
