14-Stage Study Lifecycle

From patient registration to final archive — every step tracked, automated, and auditable. WebSocket real-time updates across all dashboards.

📝

1. Registered

Patient & study created at reception. Demographics, modality, referring doctor, billing captured. PCPNDT Form F auto-triggered for ultrasound.

⏳

2. Waiting

Patient in queue. Technician sees prioritized worklist. SMS/WhatsApp appointment reminders sent. Estimated wait time displayed.

✅

3. Checked-In

Patient arrives, identity verified (ABHA/Aadhaar). Consent captured. Study assigned to modality room. Preparation instructions sent.

📡

4. Acquired

Modality sends images via DICOM C-STORE. DicoFlow DICOM SCP receives & routes. Automatic DICOM header reconciliation with worklist.

📥

5. Received

Images stored, indexed, checksummed. Technician QA: verify slice count, coverage, patient positioning. Flag for re-scan if needed.

👁️

6. Under Review

Radiologist opens in DICOM viewer. Prior studies auto-loaded. Hanging protocol applied. AI inference runs in background (T4).

📄

7. Report Drafted

Radiologist dictates findings (Whisper voice) or uses templates. Structured reporting per modality. Key measurements auto-populated.

✍️

8. Verified

Senior radiologist reviews & co-signs. Digital signature applied. Report locked — no further edits without addendum workflow.

📖

9. Booklet Generated

Dispatcher creates branded 4-page booklet. Cover page, DICOM images, report, back cover. Live PDF preview before generation.

🖨️

10. Printed

Film preparation + booklet print. Print queue managed. DICOM Print SCU for dry-film printers. PDF for regular printers.

📲

11. Shared

WhatsApp / email delivery to patient & referring doctor. Secure link with time-limited access. QR code on printed booklet.

📨

12. Submitted

Insurance / TPA claim submitted. Supporting documents attached. Claim tracking dashboard. Auto-follow-up on pending claims.

💰

13. Paid

Payment reconciled. GST invoice finalized. Doctor commission calculated. Revenue recognized. Outstanding flagged for collection.

🗄️

14. Archived

Study moved to long-term archive. Lossless compression applied. Checksum verified. Retrievable for comparison with future studies.
