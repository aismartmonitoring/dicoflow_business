12-Week Sales Launch Plan

Week-by-week activity plan, pitch scripts per tier, objection handling, demo strategy, and closing techniques. Everything your sales team needs to hit the ground running.

Launch Timeline

12-Week Go-to-Market Plan

1

Week 1-2: Foundation

Objective: Team enablement & territory mapping

  • Sales team product training (all 5 tiers)
  • Competitive battle cards preparation
  • Territory mapping: list all diagnostic centres within 50 km radius
  • CRM setup with centre profiles (modalities, size, current vendor)
  • Demo environment setup & rehearsal
  • LinkedIn profiles optimized for healthcare IT
Target: 200 centres identified & profiled
2

Week 3-4: Outreach Blitz

Objective: First contact with 100+ centres

  • Cold calling: 25 centres/day per rep
  • WhatsApp Business outreach with product brochure
  • Email campaigns (segmented by tier/modality)
  • LinkedIn InMail to centre owners & managers
  • Google Ads launch: "PACS software India", "diagnostic billing software"
  • First 10 demo bookings target
Target: 100 contacts, 15 demos booked
3

Week 5-6: Demo & Pipeline

Objective: Demonstrate product, build pipeline

  • 30-minute live demos (remote via Zoom or on-site)
  • Tier-specific demo scripts (see below)
  • ROI calculator walkthrough during every demo
  • Free 7-day trial offer for serious prospects
  • Referral programme launch (₹10K per conversion)
  • Follow-up within 48 hours of every demo
Target: 20 demos done, 5 proposals sent
4

Week 7-8: First Conversions

Objective: Close first 3-5 deals

  • Proposal follow-up with urgency (Early Adopter spots filling)
  • On-site visits for high-value prospects (T3/T4)
  • Case study preparation from trial users
  • Volume discount negotiation for multi-site prospects
  • Payment plan discussion (50% advance model)
  • Contract & implementation scheduling
Target: 3-5 signed contracts
5

Week 9-10: Scale & Events

Objective: Scale outreach, attend industry events

  • Attend local IRIA chapter meeting with demo laptop
  • Radiology conference booth (if timing aligns)
  • Webinar: "Going Paperless: ROI of Digital Diagnostics"
  • Customer success story published (with permission)
  • Google Ads optimization (negative keywords, bid adjustment)
  • SEO content: blog posts on PCPNDT compliance, film costs, PACS comparison
Target: 50 new leads from events + digital
6

Week 11-12: Accelerate

Objective: Hit 10+ cumulative sales, optimize funnel

  • Pipeline review: identify stalled deals, re-engage
  • Customer referral follow-up (₹10K reward processing)
  • Quarterly review of sales metrics & conversion rates
  • Funnel optimization: which channels convert best?
  • Expand territory to adjacent cities
  • Plan for Month 4-6 growth targets
Target: 10+ cumulative deals, ₹20L+ pipeline
Pitch Scripts

5-Minute Pitch Per Tier

🏥 Tier 1 Pitch — Clinic Owner (₹75K) ▼

Opening: "Doctor sahab, how do you currently manage your PCPNDT Form F records? And your GST invoicing — is it done manually or through Tally?"

Pain Point: "Most clinics I visit are spending 2-3 hours daily on manual billing and Form F. That's time you could spend with patients. And one PCPNDT inspection with incomplete records means ₹5 lakh fine and possible licence suspension."

Solution: "DicoFlow automates all of this. Form F is auto-generated for every ultrasound referral. GST invoices go directly to NIC — no Tally needed. Patient registration, billing, receipts, doctor commissions — all in one system."

Close: "It's ₹75,000 one-time. With the billing leakage recovery alone — most clinics lose 10-15% to missed charges — it pays for itself in 2-3 months. And right now, as an early adopter, you get 30% off — that's just ₹52,500."

🖥️ Tier 2 Pitch — Imaging Centre Manager (₹1.5L) ▼

Opening: "How much are you spending on film every month? X-ray, CT, MRI — all combined?"

Pain Point: "₹1.5L-4L per month on film. That's ₹18-48 lakh per year — just printed, handed to patients, and thrown away. Plus, you're losing diagnostic capability — you can't measure, annotate, or magnify on film."

Solution: "DicoFlow gives you a professional DICOM viewer with 50+ tools — measurements, ROIs, specialty tools like CTR for chest X-rays. Film preparation for when you DO need to print — 10+ layouts, per-slot W/L control, 600 DPI. Patient CD/USB burning. Full PACS archival."

Close: "At ₹1.5L one-time, your film savings alone pay it back in under 2 months. And you get 30% off as an early adopter — just ₹1.05L. Compare that to MedSynapse at ₹3.5L+ or PostDICOM at ₹3L+."

📝 Tier 3 Pitch — Radiology HOD (₹3.5L) ▼

Opening: "How are your radiologists currently dictating reports? And how do you deliver reports to patients — paper or digital?"

Pain Point: "Manual report typing means inconsistency across radiologists. Paper-only delivery means no branding, no professional booklet, and patients lose reports. Your dispatchers are spending hours on manual print and delivery."

Solution: "DicoFlow Tier 3 adds voice dictation (Whisper AI — works offline), structured report templates per modality, and our unique booklet system — 4-page branded reports with your logo, DICOM images, radiologist findings, and back cover. 101 configurable fields. Live PDF preview."

Close: "₹3.5L one-time includes everything in the PACS tier plus radiology workflow. No competitor offers branded booklets — it's a DicoFlow exclusive. At 30% early adopter discount: ₹2.45L."

🏗️ Tier 4 Pitch — Hospital CIO (₹7.5L) ▼

Opening: "How many branches do you manage? And what's your current HIS — do you have HL7 or FHIR integration with your imaging system?"

Pain Point: "Multi-branch hospitals using separate PACS per site have zero cross-branch visibility. Studies don't follow patients. SLAs are unmonitored. IT staff are firefighting instead of innovating."

Solution: "DicoFlow Enterprise gives you centralized multi-branch management, HL7/FHIR bidirectional integration, AI inference (ONNX Runtime — lung nodule detection included), SLA management with escalation, and our unique self-healing architecture — 7 subsystems that auto-recover without IT intervention."

Close: "₹7.5L one-time vs ₹15-25L from MedSynapse or international vendors. 70+ role-based permissions, WebSocket real-time dashboards, and immutable audit trail for NABH accreditation. At 30% early adopter: ₹5.25L."

🔗 Tier 5 Pitch — Multi-Modality Clinic (₹2.25L) ▼

Opening: "You have X-ray and ultrasound plus billing — are these on the same system currently, or separate software?"

Pain Point: "Separate billing and PACS means double data entry. Patient registered in billing, again in PACS. Revenue reports don't include imaging data. Two vendors, two AMCs, two support lines."

Solution: "Tier 5 combines full Billing (T1) + full PACS (T2) in one unified system. One patient record. One dashboard. Billing auto-triggered on study completion. Cross-module reporting. Single AMC of ₹40K instead of ₹45K separate."

Close: "₹2.25L one-time for both. Same as buying separately, but with unified integration. Early adopter: ₹1.57L. And when you grow to need reporting — upgrade to T3 for just the differential."

Objection Handling

Top 10 Objections & Responses

💰 "It's too expensive"▼

"Let me run the ROI calculator with your numbers. Most centres see payback in 2-3 months from film savings and billing recovery alone. Compare: MedSynapse charges ₹3.5L+ for just PACS — we're ₹1.5L. And this is a one-time purchase, not annual."

🤔 "We already have a system"▼

"Great — what does it do for PCPNDT compliance? GST e-Invoice? Does it have 50+ DICOM tools? Branded booklets? Immutable hash chain? Most existing systems cover 30% of what DicoFlow does. And we handle data migration from your current system."

🕐 "We'll think about it"▼

"Absolutely. But keep in mind — the Early Adopter programme is limited to 50 customers and spots are filling. The 30% discount won't be available later. Can I schedule a follow-up for next week with a 7-day trial access?"

☁️ "We want cloud-based"▼

"We support hybrid deployment — primary on-premise + cloud backup. Full cloud is also available through our managed hosting. But consider: DICOM images are huge (100-500 MB per CT study). Viewing over cloud has latency. On-premise gives instant access. Plus, your data stays with you — no DPDP Act concerns about third-party hosting."

🛠️ "What about support?"▼

"AMC includes unlimited bug fixes, version upgrades, phone + remote support (Mon-Sat 9AM-6PM). Critical issues: 4-hour response. Our self-healing architecture (Tier 4) means most issues are auto-resolved before you even notice. On-site visits are available at cost."

🔒 "Is the data secure?"▼

"Bank-grade. AES-256-GCM envelope encryption for all patient data. Ed25519 JWT tokens. Argon2id password hashing. Immutable SHA-512 hash chain with Ed25519 checkpoint signatures — that's a level of security most banks don't have. And everything runs on-premise — your data never leaves your building."

🏢 "Can it integrate with our HIS?"▼

"Yes — Tier 4 includes HL7 v2.x MLLP and FHIR R4 REST API. We support ADT, ORM, ORU message types. DICOMweb (WADO-RS, QIDO-RS, STOW-RS) for imaging interop. We've integrated with major HIS vendors in India."

📈 "We're too small, we'll grow into it"▼

"That's exactly why we have 5 tiers. Start with T1 Billing at ₹75K. When you add imaging, upgrade to T2 for just ₹75K more. Your data, settings, everything carries over. You only ever pay the differential."

🇮🇳 "International PACS vendors are better"▼

"International vendors don't have PCPNDT Form F. They don't have GST e-Invoice. They don't have ABHA ID. They don't understand Indian pricing — they charge ₹15-40L for enterprise. DicoFlow is built for Indian regulations, Indian workflows, and Indian budgets."

🆓 "Can we try before we buy?"▼

"Absolutely. We offer a 7-day trial with your own data. Full functionality. If you're not convinced it pays for itself within 6 months, we'll discuss. But in 3 years and hundreds of demos, no one has returned it."

Demo Strategy

30-Minute Demo Flow

1

Minutes 0-5: Discovery

Ask about current system, pain points, modalities, staff count, film cost. Qualify the tier recommendation.

2

Minutes 5-10: Pain Amplification

Calculate their annual film cost. Show compliance risk (PCPNDT ₹5L fine). Quantify billing leakage (10-15% of revenue).

3

Minutes 10-20: Live Product Demo

Show their recommended tier in action. Focus on their top 3 pain points. Film prep, viewer tools, or booklets — whatever resonates.

4

Minutes 20-25: ROI Walkthrough

Open roi-calculator.html. Enter their actual numbers. Show payback period. Compare with competitor pricing.

5

Minutes 25-30: Close

Early Adopter offer (30% off, limited spots). 7-day trial offer. Schedule follow-up within 48 hours. Ask for the order.
