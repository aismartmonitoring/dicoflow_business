Customer Lifecycle

The 7-Stage Customer Journey

From first touch to multi-year advocate. Every stage has specific actions and KPIs.

1

Lead Capture

Google Ads, cold calls, WhatsApp, trade shows, referrals. Target: 50-100 leads/month. KPI: Cost per lead ₹200-500

2

Qualification & Demo

Qualify needs (tier match), schedule 30-min demo. Film cost calculator + ROI calculator. KPI: 25-30% lead→demo conversion

3

Proposal & Close

Send customised proposal within 24 hours. Follow up at 24h, 48h, 7 days. Early Adopter urgency. KPI: 25-35% demo→close conversion

4

Implementation (7-14 days)

On-site setup, DICOM router config, training, data migration. First 7 days = critical satisfaction window. KPI: Go-live within 10 days

5

Adoption & Health Check (Day 30-90)

Monthly check-in calls. Feature adoption tracking. Resolve any friction. NPS survey at Day 60. KPI: NPS ≥ 8, 80%+ feature adoption

6

Upsell & Expand (Month 4-10)

Identify upsell opportunity based on usage. T1→T2, T2→T3/T5, T3→T4. Multi-branch expansion. KPI: 30-40% of customers upgrade within 12 months

7

Renew & Refer (Month 10+)

AMC renewal conversation starts Month 10. Ask for 2-3 referrals. Case study permission. KPI: 95% AMC renewal rate, 2 referrals per customer

AMC Renewal Playbook

Maximise 95% Renewal Rate

AMC is 85% margin pure recurring revenue. Every lost renewal = ₹15K-1.25L/year gone forever. Follow this exactly.

📅 Month 10: Seed the Conversation

During regular check-in: "Your AMC renewal is coming up in 2 months. We'll prepare a renewal summary showing all the support tickets we resolved, updates installed, and new features added this year." Plant the value seed early.

📊 Month 11: Value Report

Send a 1-page PDF: (1) # of support tickets resolved, (2) Software updates installed, (3) New features they received, (4) Uptime %, (5) Data backed up safely. Make renewal feel like a no-brainer. Include renewal quote.

💰 Month 11.5: Early Renewal Incentive

"Renew before expiry and get 1 extra month of coverage free (13 months for price of 12)." Creates urgency. This costs you almost nothing but prevents lapses.

🔄 Month 12: Close or Escalate

If not renewed by expiry: (1) Call decision maker directly, (2) Offer 2-month grace period with full support, (3) Explain what happens without AMC: no updates, no support, security patches stop. Most will renew when they realize the risk.

📈 During Renewal: Upsell Attempt

Every renewal is an upsell opportunity. "Since you're renewing, want to see what T3 offers? It's just ₹2L more and would automate your reporting completely." 30% of renewals convert an upsell if timed right.

🎁 Year 2+ Loyalty Benefits

Multi-year customers get: (1) Priority support (4hr SLA vs 24hr), (2) Free additional user licences (up to 2), (3) Beta access to new features, (4) Annual health check visit. These cost almost nothing but massively reduce churn.

Customer Health Metrics

KPIs to Track Monthly

Net Promoter Score (NPS)
≥ 50

Survey every 6 months. 9-10 = Promoter (ask for referral). 7-8 = Passive (upsell opportunity). ≤6 = Detractor (immediate intervention).

AMC Renewal Rate
95%+

Below 90% = systemic problem. Investigate top 3 churn reasons. Industry benchmark: 85%. Our target: 95%.

Time to First Value
≤ 7 days

From contract signing to first patient billed/first study archived. Faster = higher satisfaction = more referrals.

Support Resolution Time
< 4 hrs

Critical issues (system down): 1 hour. High: 4 hours. Medium: 24 hours. Low: 48 hours. Track and report monthly.

Feature Adoption Rate
80%+

% of purchased features actually used. Low adoption = churn risk. Offer training if below 60%.

Referral Rate
2 per customer

Ask every happy customer for 2-3 referrals. Pay ₹10K per conversion. Cheapest acquisition channel (₹10K CAC).

Upsell Rate
30-40%

% of eligible customers (6+ months) who upgrade. T1→T2 highest (35-45%), T3→T4 lowest (15-25%).

Customer Lifetime Value
₹5.2L avg

Licence + 5yr AMC + upsell avg. Must be ≥10x CAC for sustainable growth. Current: 15-25x.

Referral Engine

How to Build a Self-Sustaining Referral Machine

🎯 When to Ask

Perfect moments: (1) Day 30 — first "wow" moment, (2) After resolving a tough support ticket fast, (3) During AMC renewal, (4) After a successful implementaion, (5) When they share positive feedback unprompted. NEVER ask during a problem.

💬 How to Ask

"[Name], we're growing DicoFlow in [city]. Do you know 2-3 other centre owners who might benefit from what we've done for you? I'd love to show them how you've reduced film costs by [X]%. Happy to mention you as a reference."

💰 Referral Incentives

For referrer: ₹10,000 cash or 1 month free AMC per converted referral. For referee: Additional 5% discount on top of Early Adopter pricing. Cost to you: ₹10-15K per deal (vs ₹15-80K CAC from ads).

📊 Referral Economics

Target: 2 referrals per happy customer. Conversion rate: 40-50% (warm leads convert 2x better). Year 1: 36 customers → 72 referrals → 30-36 qualified leads → 12-18 deals. Referral revenue Year 1: ₹25-40L.
