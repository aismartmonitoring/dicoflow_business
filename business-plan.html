🎯 First 60 Days — Critical Launch Phase

Week-by-Week Milestones

Primary focus: T2 PACS (highest volume) and T4 Enterprise (highest value). These two tiers drive 70% of Year 1 revenue.

Week 1
Foundation & Territory Mapping
• Map all CT/MRI centres within 100 km radius (target 50+ T2 prospects)
• Identify top 10 hospital chains for T4 enterprise outreach
• Complete product training: T2 PACS demo flow + T4 Enterprise demo flow
• Set up CRM with centre profiles, modality type, current vendor
KPI: 50 T2 + 10 T4 prospects identified
Week 2
T2 PACS Blitz — Cold Outreach
• 25 cold calls/day to CT/MRI centres (25 × 5 = 125 contacts)
• WhatsApp Business: send film cost savings calculator to centre managers
• LinkedIn: connect with 50 radiology centre owners/managers
• Book first 5 demos for Week 3
KPI: 125 contacts, 8 demos booked (T2)
Week 3
T2 Demos + T4 Introduction
• Execute 5-8 T2 PACS demos (30 min: pain → film cost → viewer → ROI)
• Send T4 Enterprise intro emails to hospital CIOs/IT heads
• WhatsApp follow-up within 24 hours of each demo
• Start Google Ads: "PACS software India" + "CT centre software"
KPI: 6 demos done, 2 proposals sent (T2)
Week 4
First T2 Close + T4 Demo Booking
• Follow up on 2 T2 proposals — push for Early Adopter close (30% off)
• Target: 1st T2 PACS deal closed (₹1.5L)
• Book 2-3 T4 Enterprise discovery meetings
• Collect first customer testimonial/case study
MILESTONE: First revenue — ₹1.5L (T2)
Week 5
T4 Enterprise Deep Dive
• 2-3 T4 on-site visits to hospitals (bring demo laptop with real DICOM data)
• Show 14-stage workflow, multi-branch, HL7/FHIR integration
• T2 pipeline: 10 more cold calls/day, book 3-4 demos
• Implement first T2 customer (parallel track)
KPI: 2 T4 demos, 2 more T2 deals in pipeline
Week 6
Acceleration — Multi-Tier
• Target: 2nd T2 PACS close (₹1.5L)
• Send T4 Enterprise proposals to 2 hospitals
• Open T1 Billing channel: approach pathology labs, small clinics
• Launch referral programme: ₹10K per conversion
MILESTONE: Cumulative ₹3L revenue
Week 7
T4 Negotiation + Pipeline Building
• T4 hospital follow-ups: address procurement, IT integration questions
• T2: 3 more demos, push pipeline deals to close
• First T1 Billing demo to clinic owners (₹75K is impulse buy territory)
• Publish first blog post: "How DicoFlow Reduces Film Costs by 70%"
KPI: T4 proposal in negotiation, 5 total T2 proposals out
Week 8
🏆 Month 2 Close — Target: ₹12L+ Cumulative
• Target: 1st T4 Enterprise close (₹7.5L) — GAME CHANGER
• Target: 3rd T2 PACS close (₹1.5L)
• T1 Billing: close 1-2 quick deals (₹75K each)
• Month 2 review: refine pitch, optimize channels
MILESTONE: ₹12-15L cumulative — approaching break-even trajectory

⚠️ Things to Avoid

Sales Traps & Tricky Situations

These mistakes can kill deals, damage reputation, or waste months. Learn them before you start.

❌ Don't Over-Discount

Early Adopter 30% off is the maximum. Never go beyond. If they push, add value (extra training, faster SLA) instead of cutting price. Once you discount beyond 30%, every future customer will expect it.

❌ Don't Sell T4 to Small Clinics

A 2-doctor X-ray clinic doesn't need enterprise. They need T1 or T2. Over-selling creates unhappy customers who feel they overpaid. Right-size every deal. Upsell later when they genuinely need it.

❌ Don't Promise Custom Features

Never promise "we'll add that feature for you" during a sale. Every custom feature delays the roadmap and creates support debt. Show what exists. If they need something specific, log it as a request — don't commit.

❌ Don't Skip the Demo

Never send a proposal without a live demo. The product sells itself when they see it. A proposal without a demo converts at 5%. With demo: 25-35%. Always book the demo first.

❌ Don't Trash Competitors Directly

Never say "MedSynapse is bad." Instead: "They're a good product — but they don't have PCPNDT automation, they're cloud-only, and they cost 2x more." Let the comparison chart do the work. Stay professional.

❌ Don't Ignore Follow-Up Timing

Follow up within 24 hours of a demo. Within 48 hours max. After 72 hours, the deal cools by 50%. After 1 week, it's almost dead. Set calendar reminders for every follow-up. Use CRM religiously.

❌ Don't Quote Infra Cost Without Profit

When quoting Azure/AWS/GCP managed hosting, ALWAYS include the 25% DicoFlow margin. Never show raw cloud pricing. The infrastructure calculator already includes this. Use the calculator in front of the client.

❌ Don't Let Implementation Slip

Promise 7-14 day implementation. Deliver in 7. Late implementation → unhappy customer → no referral → no case study. The first 10 customers are your marketing engine. Treat them like gold.

❌ Don't Ignore Government Red Tape (T4)

Hospital chains and government hospitals have procurement processes: tender, committee, 3-quote comparison, PO approval. This takes 2-6 months. Factor it into the T4 pipeline. Start early, be patient.
